How can we help you?

Everything you need to know

If you’ve already purchased your pass, just go to thompsonboxing.com where you purchased your pay per view pass. You will be able to click on that event when it begins and continue to enjoy your purchase.

If you are not already logged in, click on the content you’ve purchased and log in using the email and password you registered with. From there you will be able to enjoy streaming.

If you haven’t purchased a pass yet, go to the thompsonboxing.com, click on the event you want to watch, log in or sign up, and proceed to make your purchase.
